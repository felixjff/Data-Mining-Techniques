-----FEATURE EXTRACTION-----

Below some features are described which can be added to the dataset.
* = already written
1. Averages of different features over the srch_id, prop_id, srch_destination_id.
2. *Price difference from previous hotel price and current price.
3. *Price rank - the order of the pricing of the hotel with respect to srch_id.
4. *Hotel position - the position of the hotel in the same destination in previous and next searches. Average of these.
5. *Hotel quality - Booking number divided by number of times the hotel appears, per prop_id. Same for clicked number.
6. *Star rank - the order of the hotel star rating with respect to the srch_id
7. *Price difference rank - the order of the price difference with respect to the srch_id
